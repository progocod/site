Our Goal

1. To equip hundreds of African youths around the continent through our leadership school and mentorship program. Also through our conferences.
2. To grant thousands of less privileged youths scholarships to study both home and abroad.

Our Vision

Our core vision is raising African youth leaders through our conferences and providing care for the less privileged.
